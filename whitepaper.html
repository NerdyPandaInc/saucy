🚀 What Makes Boom Inu Different

1. The Flip Streak Mechanic

Unlike tokens like $SHIB, $DOGE, or $BONK, Boom Inu rewards active participation. Flip (trade in and out) with sufficient volume to build a streak. Achieving a streak triggers temporary double reflection rewards—creating a game-like economy of traders racing to maintain streaks.

2. Randomized Reflection Distribution

Reflections are pseudo-randomly claimable by eligible holders, adding gamification and viral energy: "Did you get your BOOM today?"

3. Automated & Public Treasury Burns

Anyone can trigger burns when treasury thresholds are exceeded—creating a trustless, transparent supply reduction system.

4. Weekend Boost Windows

Special reflection windows open automatically on weekends to increase activity—no centralized decisions needed. The code runs the rhythm.
